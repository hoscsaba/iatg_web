Serial and Parallel Throttling


    Run demo
    (To run this application Java Runtime Environment is necessary. www.oracle.com)
In case of serial throttling, the throttle valve is connected in series between the hydraulic charge unit and the motor or machine and in the case of parallel throttling, the throttle valve is connetcted in parallel. the type of throttling can influence the operation of the hydraulic charge unit.

In the Demo, the black point represents the operating point of the hydraulic charge unit (pump + pressure relief valve), green is the pump's operating point, the area delimited by blue is the engine's performance, the red lines indicate the THROTTLE.

In the case of serial throttling, the FLOW VOLUME provided by the hydraulic charge unit is the same, but the PRESSURE is divided between the throttle and the motor, so to get the same power from the motor, higher pressure must be provided than in the case of parallel throttling.

For parallel throttling, the PRESSURE on the motor and the throttle is the same, but the FLOW VOLUME is divided. The losses in a serial throttling setup are higher than in a parallel system so the efficiency of parallel throttling is higher than that of the serial throttling.
